Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Email address (for your account). Your preferences (goal, wake time, session length, injury areas). Daily stress check-ins. Session completion data. Post-session feedback.

How we use it

To generate personalized sessions. To track your progress over time. To improve LiveNew. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

AI processing

Your check-in data (stress level, energy, sleep) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your sessions. This data is not stored by Anthropic after processing. No personally identifiable information is included in AI requests.

Where your data lives

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use HTTPS for all connections.

Deleting your data

Email support@livenew.app and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies. No analytics cookies.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email support@livenew.app.
