Help Center

What is LiveNew?

LiveNew is a daily stress regulation app. It builds you a personalized plan each day — morning plan, a midday plan, and an evening plan — all generated by AI based on how you're feeling.

How does it work?

When you open the app, tell us how you're feeling. LiveNew generates your day plan in seconds. Each session guides you through specific physical techniques — positions, movements, and breathing patterns — designed to lower your stress. Follow the instructions on screen. That's it.

How long are the sessions?

You choose during setup — 10, 15, or 20 minutes per session. Your midday plan includes a 5-minute guided midday plan. You can change your session length anytime in your account settings.

Can I change my preferences?

Yes. Tap Account in the top right corner to change your goal, wake time, session length, or injury areas. Your next day plan will reflect the changes.
