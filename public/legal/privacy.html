Privacy Policy

Effective: January 27, 2026

LiveNew collects only the data needed to generate your daily plan and keep the service reliable.

What we collect

  • Account identifiers (email) for authentication.
  • Profile and check-in inputs used to tailor plans.
  • Completion and feedback signals to improve quality.
  • Technical logs for reliability and abuse protection.

How we use data

  • Generate and improve daily plans.
  • Provide support and debug issues.
  • Protect the service from abuse.

Your choices

  • You can export or delete your data from the profile page.
  • Data minimization settings control how much history is retained.

For questions, contact support through the app.
